Developer / Tinkerer / Absurdist

I am a full stack developer with a passion for building fast, scalable systems and simplifying complex workflows.

My journey with code began during my undergraduate years, and I haven’t looked back since. I love solving real-world problems through thoughtful design, clean architecture, and meaningful user experiences. From scripting my first backend service to deploying microservices in production, I’ve thrived in shipping practical solutions that serve thousands.

Over the years, I’ve worked across diverse domains—from education tech to high-performance sales platforms. Currently, I’m a Senior Full Stack Engineer at Poshn, where I help architect and maintain lead capture and distribution systems across high-traffic channels like Typeform and Facebook Ads. My tech stack is diverse—Node.js, React, FastAPI, MongoDB, AWS, and more—and I enjoy learning new tools that align with purpose-driven engineering.

Before this, I built CMS portals, test platforms, and scheduling systems at Ufaber, and optimized data systems at Kyndryl. Across every role, the goal has remained consistent: build performant, reliable, and developer-friendly systems that scale.

I’m particularly drawn to developer experience, automation, and the interplay of frontend and backend in creating seamless applications. When I’m not coding, I’m probably debugging Docker containers or refining a deployment pipeline that didn’t need refinement.

This website is a reflection of both my professional work and personal side projects. You’ll find links to live applications, source code, and writeups about things I’ve learned and built over the years. This site evolves as I do, but the core idea remains the same: build, learn, and share.
